Keuka Lake State Park

Keuka Lake State Park is perfect for a day of relaxation on a lake. You can go fishing, swimming, boating, hunting, and hiking.

The park is fairly small at only 2.5 kilometers in size. However, the park does contain a small cemetery belonging to the Beddoe-Rose Family.

The park has facilities for camping and picnics.

Keuka lake has excellent options for water activities while being light on hiking. The camping sites are of high quality as well.
