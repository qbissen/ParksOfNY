Buttermilk Falls

Buttermilk falls offers a small lake in the upper park and hiking trails throughout the gorge. There is also pools of water in the lower park along with camping and a wetland.

The park also offers around ten different waterfalls with Buttermilk Falls being the largest of them. The primary trail is the Gorge trail which follows Buttermilk Creek with the secondary Rim Trail on the other side of the creek. Finally, there is the beginning of the Finger Lakes Trail.

It is recommended for hikers to bring swimwear and sandals as there are many occasions to cool off in some of the natural pools created by the creek.
